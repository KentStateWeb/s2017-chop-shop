About Us

Founded in 2008, the Ohio Chop Shop was born from owner Ana’s passion for the fashion and beauty industry. The vision was simple, offer world class salon and spa services in an upbeat modern atmosphere, while respecting our environment. The concept was a hit, and Ohio Chop Shop was fast on it’s way to making waves in the Lancaster beauty industry.
